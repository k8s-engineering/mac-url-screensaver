# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful in communication.
- Give actionable, technical feedback.
- Assume good intent and clarify misunderstandings quickly.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and users.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, intimidation, or doxxing
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributors for conduct that harms the community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.
